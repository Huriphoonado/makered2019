Accelerometor Activity

1

Download

Download an accelerometor app to your phone that supports JSON export. We use this free one found in the App Store.

2

Move

Ensure the accelerometer is tracking data and record your motions. Short recordings less then ten seconds work best.

3

Capture

Save your recording and email it to yourself as a JSON file.

4

Explore

Open your email, then copy and paste your data to the text area on the right. Scroll down to view valid data as a plot.

AMPED Research Lab

AMPED (Analytical Methods for Progressive Educational Design), is dedicated to developing methodology to fill gaps in learning and assessment design. The application areas we focus on for methodology are collaboration, problem-solving processes, and project-oriented learning spaces such as Fab Labs and Makerspaces.
